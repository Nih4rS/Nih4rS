Case Study

Cloud & Sustainable Systems

Pairing AWS-native architectures with LSTM forecasting to optimize EV charging and trading pipelines.

Back to projects Previous project

EV Charging Intelligence

  • Designed an event-driven architecture with AWS IoT Core ingesting telemetry from charge points.
  • Used AWS Lambda for lightweight transformations and DynamoDB for session storage with TTL-based retention.
  • Trained SageMaker LSTM/Seq2Seq models to forecast load, informing dispatch schedules that balanced grid costs and sustainability goals.
  • Packaged dashboards in QuickSight for utility partners, highlighting KPIs such as kWh saved and CO2 avoided.

Algorithmic Trading ETL

  • Built Python ETL services that streamed market data via REST + WebSocket APIs and landed cleaned feeds in Kafka topics.
  • Leveraged TensorFlow-based LSTM models with rolling retraining to forecast price movements and volatility regimes.
  • Implemented risk overlays (VaR-style thresholds) and alerting hooks for human-in-the-loop validation.
  • Produced monitoring notebooks and lightweight dashboards to visualize drift, forecast accuracy, and Sharpe-style metrics.

Key Takeaways

  • Cloud-native design patterns transfer seamlessly between sustainability and fintech contexts.
  • Explainability artifacts (feature importance, scenario planners) are essential for both utility partners and traders.
  • Hybrid data skill sets–hardware telemetry, ML, BI–accelerate delivery for multidisciplinary teams.
